Rising Upwords
A word ladders game that pushes players vocabularies to the test. Utilizes problem solving skills to get from one word to another by only changing one letter at a time.

Installation
To play the game just click on the Rising Upwords Icon.

How to Play
1. Select either singleplayer or multiplayer mode
2. Choose the difficulty level (3, 4, or 5 letter words)
3. Click the play button to start the game
4. Two words will be shown, one at the top and bottom
5. Type in words that get from the bottom word to the top word by using real words and only changing one letter at a time
6. If your answer is correct, you will score a point and move on to the next set of words. Otherwise you will be deducted an attempt.

Features
-Singleplayer or multiplayer mode
-Three different difficulty levels
-Leaderboard to track your scores and progress
-Toggleable sounds

Known Issues
-Not all difficulties are able to get from one word to the next
