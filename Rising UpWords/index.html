How to play

  1. 1. Type in words to get from the word below to the word at the top and press enter.
  2. 2. You can not enter words with more or less than a 1 letter difference to the word below.
  3. 3. You can only input real words.
  4. 4. If you fail to input a valid word, you will be deducted an attempt.
  5. 5. When you reach the word at the top, you will be awarded a point.
  6. 6. Try to get as many points as you can without losing all your attempts!
